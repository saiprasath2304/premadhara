WHY PREMADHARA?

We envision a world where preventive health is the cornerstone of global well-being.
Lifestyle diseases like diabetes, heart disease, and obesity are escalating worldwide, leading to immense personal suffering and overwhelming healthcare costs.
Cancer rates continue to climb, with many cases preventable through early detection and lifestyle change.
Traditional healthcare models often emphasise treatment over prevention, placing a strain on resources and limiting options for long-term health.
